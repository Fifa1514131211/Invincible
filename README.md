# Invincible
Invincible is a fan-made knowledge game inspired by 82-0.com.

How to play
Spin. Each round the machine lands on a club and an era.
Pick. That squad appears — choose one player. Positions are real: Ronaldo is a RW at Man Utd, a LW at Real Madrid.
Place. Click your player, then a glowing spot on the pitch. Twelve rounds build a 4-3-3 plus a super sub.
Survive the season. Your twelve earn a team score out of 100 — quality and balance, so one weak link costs you. Score high enough and you win every game: Invincible.
One team skip and one era skip per game — spend them well. Card numbers are stylised era averages; the engine's true ratings stay hidden.
