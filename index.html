How to play
  1. Spin. Each round the machine lands on a club and an era.
  2. Pick. That squad appears — choose one player. Positions are real: Ronaldo is a RW at Man Utd, a LW at Real Madrid.
  3. Place. Click your player, then a glowing spot on the pitch. Twelve rounds build a 4-3-3 plus a super sub.
  4. Survive the season. Your twelve earn a team score out of 100 — quality and balance, so one weak link costs you. Score high enough and you win every game: Invincible.
One team skip and one era skip per game — spend them well. Card numbers are stylised era averages; the engine's true ratings stay hidden.
